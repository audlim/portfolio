How to keep track of the number of codes developers are committing?

This dashboard was created to solve the pain point of my colleagues in the India team, who were having difficulty keeping track of the code output of their developers. Pulling data from an internal system, I created views that enabled users to drill down into the number of codes committed according to the developer/non-developer job groups, with further analysis according to the job grade of employees. Monthly and quarterly trend views were also created. The dashboard enabled insightful analysis into employee output and allowed for data-driven decisions to be made to influence behvioural changes.
