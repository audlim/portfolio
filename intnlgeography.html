International Geography series - Window to World Geography

This series was an adaptation of an existing product that we were commissioned to produce for the national system of army-run public schools in Pakistan. A complicated project from the get-go, I led a team of editors and designers that had to overcome challenges such as the lack of clarity on syllabus requirements and constantly changing client requests. Due to the complexity and tight schedule of the project, there was a need to scope the project into smaller pieces and bring on multiple authors to take up different parts of the work. This meant that I had to manage communications with the authors while ensuring consistency and standardisation of the work I received from them. Despite the challenges, we managed to deliver the project on time, impressing our clients and leading to them seek future opportunities with us.
